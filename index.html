A tool for teachers and educators to make creating, sharing, and grading quizzes faster and easier. From large classrooms down to one-on-one teaching, this is the tool you need.

About

Creating quizzes, exams, and worksheets is a universal pain that everyone in education experiences. Math Facts for Staples is here to help! Simplifying the process of creating and grading math tests by automatically grading quizzes and worksheets.

Why Staples?

Originally, this project was made for my good friend and teacher, Mr. Staples. He teaches in elementary school and wanted something to help him give out electronic tests to his students without making it "gameified" like many other quiz softwares do.
